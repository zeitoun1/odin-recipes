Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for making a margherita pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. preheat oven
  2. prepare dough
  3. add tomato sauce
  4. add mozzarella
  5. bake
  6. add basil, olive oil and salt
