Fried Chicken

Description

This is a recipe for making fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Buttermilk
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Vegetable oil
  • All-purpose flour
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. marinate the chicken
  2. prepare the coating
  3. dredge the chicken
  4. heat oil and fry chicken
